Frequently Asked Questions

What does "Become Featured" mean?

When you choose to "Become Featured," your profile will be highlighted on specific days within the app. This boosts your visibility, ensuring that recruiters and employers are more likely to come across your profile, increasing your chances of landing a job.

How does the "Become Featured" service help me get hired?

By becoming featured, your profile will be prioritized and shown to a larger audience of employers, making it stand out among the many candidates. The increased exposure can result in more job opportunities and higher chances of securing interviews.

How often will my profile be featured?

Your profile will be featured on selected days, as per the plan you choose. The frequency of being featured will depend on the specific package or service you select, giving you multiple opportunities to be noticed by recruiters.

Can I choose the days my profile is featured?

While we offer some flexibility, the specific days on which your profile will be featured are pre-determined to ensure optimal exposure. However, we aim to align these days with peak job-seeking activity, maximizing your visibility to recruiters.
